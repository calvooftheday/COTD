About the Project

What is COTD?

Every day, one extraordinary bald individual is chosen and celebrated. This is not about lack of hair — it is about presence, confidence, and the magnificent power of a bare scalp shining in the light of the world.

COTD — Calvo of the Day — is a daily homage to the chronically hairless and gloriously smooth-headed among us. From legend to neighbour, no calvo is too great or too humble for the spotlight.

Nominations, suggestions, and fan mail can be directed to whoever runs this thing. You know who you are.
